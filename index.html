Hello! I'm Scott

I'm currently in graduate school getting a Master's degree in History & Economics. I'm learning public policy, statistical analysis, and computer programming. I expect to finish my degree in the fall of 2018.

From 2013 to 2016, I worked at the Massachusetts State House as the Chief of Staff for the Committee on Higher Education. I managed the Chairman's legislative priorities, including researching and drafting new bills. I also advised him on political strategy, such as finding a way to move bills forward through the famously impenetrable legislative process. I also managed a three-person staff, whose responsibilities included constituent services, media relations, and committee legislation.

I graduated with a bachelor's degree in Magazine Journalism in 2006 from Syracuse University's Newhouse School of Public Communications. At Syracuse, I was a beat reporter, columnist, and the sports editor for the independent student-run newspaper The Daily Orange. I had internships at The Denver Post, The Buffalo News, and The Post-Standard.
